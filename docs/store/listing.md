## Full description

**Block Blast with a heartbeat.**

Riptide is the block puzzle you already know how to play — drag pieces, fill
rows, clear them — with one beautiful, menacing twist: **the water is rising.**

Every piece you place ticks the tide forward. Every row you clear pushes it
back. No clocks, no timers, no pressure but the ocean itself — take a breath,
read the board, and hold back the sea one perfect move at a time.

🌊 **THE TIDE IS THE VILLAIN**
Blocks the water swallows turn to dead coral — lost forever. Watch the
waterline. Time your clears. The drain moment never stops feeling good.

🐢 **RESCUE THE TIDEPOOL**
Crabs, turtles, octopuses and friends get trapped in the grid. Clear their row
before the water takes them, and they'll join your Tidepool — a little
sanctuary that grows with every rescue.

⭐ **THREE WAYS TO PLAY**
• **Voyage** — 200 hand-tuned levels across 10 zones, from sunlit tidepools to
the abyss.
• **Endless Tide** — survive an ocean that keeps accelerating. How many tides
can you outlast?
• **Daily Riptide** — one board, the whole world, one attempt. Keep your
streak alive and share your result with friends.

🧩 Easy to learn, genuinely strategic, fair by design — every board is
beatable, every loss is yours.

Free to play with optional ads and a single Remove Ads purchase. No energy
systems. No paywalls. Just you and the sea.
