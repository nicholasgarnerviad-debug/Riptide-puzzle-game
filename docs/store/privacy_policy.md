# Riptide — Privacy Policy

**Effective date: 2026-06-11 · applies to the Riptide mobile game (com.riptide.game)**

## The short version
Riptide has no accounts, collects no names, emails, or contacts, and never
sells data. The game stores your progress on your device. Two third-party
services receive limited technical data: Google AdMob (to show ads) and
Firebase Analytics (to understand aggregate gameplay).

## Data stored on your device only
Game progress (levels, stars, coins, streaks, rescued creatures, settings) is
saved locally on your device and is never uploaded by us. Deleting the app
deletes this data.

## Data processed by third parties

**Google AdMob (advertising).** When ads are enabled, AdMob may process your
advertising ID, IP address, and device information to serve and measure ads,
subject to your consent choices. In regions where consent is required, the
game shows Google's UMP consent form before any ad code initializes, and you
can change your choices anytime in Settings → Privacy options. Purchasing
Remove Ads disables interstitial advertising entirely.
Google's policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

**Firebase Analytics (gameplay statistics).** We log anonymous gameplay events
(for example: a level was completed, a daily was attempted, an ad was shown)
with device-class technical data, to balance the game and fix problems. These
events contain no personal identifiers entered by you.
Firebase terms: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

## Purchases
The optional Remove Ads purchase is processed entirely by Google Play. We
receive a confirmation token, never your payment details.

## Children
Riptide is not directed at children. The game uses a neutral age screen and
follows Google Play Families ad-content requirements where applicable.

## Your choices
- Re-open the ad consent form anytime: Settings → Privacy options.
- Reset your advertising ID or opt out of personalization in Android settings.
- Delete all local data by uninstalling the app.

## Contact
Questions about this policy: privacy@riptide.game

We will update this page when the policy changes; material changes are
announced in-app.
